But the experience seems to indicate that in recent years, the rate of employment growth has been inadequate vs. the growth of labor force and that the amount of employment generated as a result of given output growth has been declining. Such a situation naturally calls for special efforts and programmes to create employment. Apart from the general inadequacy of employment growth,South Africa faces many challenges such as child-headed households,crime,drugs etc. Our aim is to eradicate youth unemployment
